## Notes

**Ecosystem coherence:** Several mattpocock skills form an interlocking system: `setup-matt-pocock-skills` → `CONTEXT.md` / `docs/adr/` → `grill-with-docs` / `diagnose` / `tdd` / `improve-codebase-architecture` / `triage`. The individual skills gain leverage from the shared domain-doc convention. Adopting individual skills without the CONTEXT.md/ADR convention delivers partial value; adopting the convention itself may be the highest-leverage move.

**Deprecated skills:** Four skills are in `deprecated/`. Three received non-Skip verdicts because the deprecation appears to reflect workflow reorganization (these concepts were absorbed into other skills), not because the ideas are bad. Read with appropriate skepticism.

**In-progress skills:** Four skills are in `in-progress/`. The three writing skills are clearly personal workflow tools. The `review` skill's two-axis parallel structure is architecturally interesting despite being incomplete.
